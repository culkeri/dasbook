The Story of DAaaS



Statistics Canada embarked on a modernization business transformation journey in 2018 to respond to the needs of citizens, private, and public sectors for evidence based business decisions and insights. This is the basis for our Data Analytics as a Service Platform initiative.

The objective is to follow Statistics Canada Modernization initiative for a user-centric service delivery, leading-edge methods and data integration, statistical capacity building & leadership, in an environment fostering sharing and collaboration, implementing a modern and flexible workplace, and access our geospatial analysis environment on the cloud.

We know that researchers, analysts, data scientists, and policy makers have unique and complex needs. They need to offer meaningful insights and make evidence-based decisions. DAaaS is a one-stop, customizable and collaborative data analytics platform powered by Statistics Canada – a platform that brings data, people, process and expertise together.

Why Use DAaaS

  • Designed to make collaborative data analytics faster, easier and more secure.
  • A single place to analyze big data, collaborate and visualize
  • Enables users to conduct modern and innovative data analysis
  • FAIR Data Infrastructure (FDI) Support
  • Provide access to advanced cartographic tooling to increased geo-analytics capacity
  • Align with corporate and federal initiatives
  • DAaaS supports Statistics Canada and external users.

    Grâce à ces services infonuagiques sécurisés, vous pouvez :

  • Accéder au poste de travail Windows Azure pour tirer avantage d’un environnement puissant et évolutif d’analyse et de traitement géospatiaux;
  • Effectuer des analyses de base et avancées, modéliser et créer des flux de travail à l’aide d’ArcPro, de QGIS ou d’ESRI Insights;
  • Utiliser l’Explorateur de stockage Microsoft Azure pour accéder aux données géospatiales à partir de votre bureau virtuel Azure;
  • Obtenir des renseignements fondés sur l’emplacement supplémentaires grâce à l’intégration de statistiques et de données géospatiales fiables de Statistique Canada;
  • Utiliser la plateforme d’analyse et de visualisation de données géospatiales ESRI Enterprise Collaborative pour :
    • créer des éléments visuels, des récits et des tableaux de bord géospatiaux interactifs,
    • partager des ensembles de données géospatiales, des modèles, des analyses et des applications de cartographie Web,
    • permettre la collaboration entre plateformes et entre projets.

    À l’heure actuelle, nos services EAG donnent accès à des informations statistiques non sensibles.

                     
    Using these secure, cloud-based services, you can:

  • Access Azure Windows desktop for powerful and scalable Geospatial processing and analysis environment.
  • Perform basic and advanced analysis, modelling and create workflows using ArcPro, QGIS or ESRI Insights.
  • Use Azure Storage Explorer to access geospatial data from your Azure Virtual Desktop.
  • Gain further location based insights with the integration of trusted Statistics Canada statistics and geospatial data,
  • Use ESRI Enterprise Collaborative geospatial analytics and visualization platform to :
    • Create interactive geospatial visuals, stories, and dashboards.
    • Share geospatial datasets, models, analysis and web mapping applications.
    • Allow for cross-platform and cross-project collaboration.

    At this time, our GEO services provide access to non-sensitive statistical information.
